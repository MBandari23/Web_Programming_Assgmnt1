The secret identity of Spider Man is "Peter Benjamin Parker". Peter is a teenage high-school student and an orphan. He was raised by his Aunt and uncle named as Mary and Ben who resides in New York City, as peter's parents Richard and Mary Parker passed away in a plane crash. Peter gains the superhuman powers as a spider and abilities after a radioactive spider bites him. The powers of superhero spiderman includes strength, reflexes, balance, endurance, coordination, and agility. Spider-sense refers to the ability of aticking to the surfaces and ceilings like a spider, in addition, an ability of precognition of danger. He has an in-build web-shooter that can create artificial spider-webs, which he uses for fighting and for swinging across the cities. Initially, he used his spider-super powers for his personal purpose, later when his uncle Ben was killed by a thief, he began to use his powers to fight crime.
